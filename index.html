Controls

  • Move Left: A
  • Move Right: D
  • Jump: W
  • Shoot: SPACE BAR
  • Shoot Shotgun: F
  • Shoot Nuke: R
  • Use Special: K

This is my first game made with Javascript.
It took about 3 days to build and was very fun to make.
The gameplay was inspired by an old game called bubble blaster
that I used to play online back in school over and over.
